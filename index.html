ABOUT US

At PCU AI Conference, we believe that AI is rapidly changing the world we live in, and it has the potential to create solutions to some of the most pressing issues we face today.

Our mission is to create a space for discussion and exploration of AI advancements and their implications, as well as to promote the responsible and ethical development and deployment of AI technologies.
